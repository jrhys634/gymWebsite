    About Us

    Welcome to Rhys' Gym, your ultimate destination for Mixed Martial Arts (MMA) training. Our gym was established in 2022 with the aim of helping individuals achieve their fitness and fighting goals through rigorous, comprehensive and professional MMA training programs.

    At Rhys' Gym, we pride ourselves on offering top-notch coaching and facilities that cater to all levels of experience and skill. Whether you are a seasoned fighter looking to sharpen your skills or a beginner looking to learn the basics, our team of expert trainers is here to help you reach your full potential.

    We offer a wide range of classes that include Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ), Muay Thai, Boxing, Wrestling, and more. Our state-of-the-art facilities feature top-quality equipment and an atmosphere that’s conducive to training hard and having fun at the same time.

    Our main priority at Rhys' Gym is to create a supportive community that fosters growth, positivity, and respect. We believe that everyone, regardless of their background, deserves a chance to meet their goals and become the best version of themselves. That’s why we strive to create an environment that’s inclusive, friendly, and welcoming to all.

    So whether you’re looking to get fit, become an MMA fighter, or just want to improve your technique, Rhys' Gym has got you covered. Join us now and discover what it means to be part of our family!
